Regulatory Urgency

Are You a NIS2 Essential or Important Entity?

The NIS2 Directive dramatically expands the scope of mandatory cybersecurity requirements across the EU. If your organisation operates in a critical sector, you are likely already obligated to implement robust security measures — or face severe penalties.

Fines up to €10 million or 2% of global annual revenue — whichever is higher

Article 21 of NIS2 mandates specific technical and organisational measures including incident handling, business continuity, supply chain security, and 24/7 monitoring. Cyberspot maps directly to these obligations.
